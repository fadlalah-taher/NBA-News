Keyonte Darnell George (born November 8, 2003) is an American professional basketball player for the Utah Jazz of the National Basketball Association (NBA). He played college basketball for the Baylor Bears. He was a consensus five star recruit and one of the top players in the 2022 class. Keyonte George, Utah Jazz (15.1%): Speaking of empowered young guards, George has assumed the command of the Utah offense even amid a veteran-laden backcourt. The Jazz trust George to run the pick and roll and pick his spots as a scorer and passer, which has led to some really fun fantasy results.
