Ochai Young Agbaji is an American professional basketball player for the Toronto Raptors of the NBA. As a senior at the University of Kansas, Agbaji was named a consensus first-team All-American and voted the Big 12 Player of the Year in 2022. He led the Jayhawks to a national championship and was named the Final Four MOP. Agbaji (knee) is available for Thursday's game against the Suns, Josh Lewenberg of TSN.ca reports. Agbaji was previously upgraded to probable, so this was an expected update. Agbaji has struggled since joining the Raptors, hitting 37.7 percent from the field and 26.7 percent from beyond the arc.
