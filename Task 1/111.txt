Zach Collins is an American professional basketball player for the San Antonio Spurs of the NBA. He played college basketball for the Gonzaga Bulldogs. He was drafted by the Sacramento Kings with the 10th overall pick in the 2017 NBA draft and was traded to the Portland Trail Blazers on draft night. During his earlier career with the team he battled many injuries causing him to only play in 154 games in his first 4 seasons. He joined the Spurs as a free agent in August 2021. 2023-24 season statistics are 11.5 points, 5.3 rebound and 3.0 assists per game.
