Jeffrey Dowtin Jr. (born May 10, 1997) is an American professional basketball player for the Philadelphia 76ers of the NBA, on a two-way contract with the Delaware Blue Coats of the NBA G League. He played college basketball for the Rhode Island Rams. Dowtin attended St. John's College, where he averaged 15.3 points and 5.0 assists per game as a senior. In 2016, he was identified as a three-star recruit by Scout, Rivals, and ESPN and was ranked the #43 point guard in the nation by ESPN. Now in 2023-24 season statistics are 5 points and 1.5 rebounds per game.
