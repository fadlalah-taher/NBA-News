Luka Doncic (35 pts, 11 reb, 11 ast) posted his fifth straight 30+ point triple-double tying Russell Westbrook for the longest streak ever to lead the Mavs to a 114-108 win over the Heat to snap a three-game slide. Kyrie Irving added 23 points, including a clutch 3 to put the Mavs up five with 1:28 to play. The Mavs hold the No. 8 spot in the Play-In, increasing their lead to 1.5 games over the Warriors. The Heat stay at the No. 6 spot in the East via a tiebreaker with Philadelphia at No. 7 (lead season series 2-0).
