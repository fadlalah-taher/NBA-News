Cameron Bouchea Thomas is an American professional basketball player for the Brooklyn Nets of the National Basketball Association. He was a consensus five-star recruit and one of the best shooting guards in the 2020 class. He is the youngest player in NBA history to score 40 points in three straight games.Thomas was born in Yokosuka, Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan while his mother was living there. When he was seven, he won a contest by making 33 free throws in a row. Cameron Thomas this season 2023-24 statistics are 20.9 points, 2.9 rebounds, 2.7 assists and 43.7 FG % per game.
