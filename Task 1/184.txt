A new era dawns with the introduction of Hawk-Eye tracking technology. The technology, which replaces the Second Spectrum's "center of mass" data, captures real-time movements, enhancing the accuracy and speed of officiating decisions. It will play a crucial role in making calls on plays such as out of bounds and goaltending. The implementation is expected to substantially reduce human error, ensuring that the outcomes of games are more fair and that each team is given a fair chance to showcase their skills and strategies. The speed of gameplay with automated and faster calls making the game more fluid and engaging.
