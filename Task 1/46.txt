NBA Play-IN Tournament; The National Basketball Association Board of Governors approved the adoption of the NBA Play-In Tournament on a full-time basis back in July 2022. In the 2023-24 NBA season, the Play-In Tournament will take place April 16-19, 2024, between the last day of the regular season on April 14 and the start of the playoffs on April 20. The tournament includes the four teams with the seventh to tenth best winning percentages in the Eastern and Western Conferences. After six games, the seventh and eighth seeds in each conference will be determined for the NBA Playoffs.
