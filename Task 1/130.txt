Jr. NBA Leagues All-Star Tournament – To celebrate the launch of Jr. NBA Leagues in Indiana, the Jr. NBA will host its first-ever Jr. NBA Leagues All-Star Tournament for 18 local youth basketball teams. The tournament begins on Saturday, Feb. 17 and will include a boy’s division and a girl’s division. The top four teams in each division will advance to the semi-finals and finals on Sunday, Feb. 18. Additional Jr. NBA activities include the Jr. NBA Coaches Clinic, Coaching Girls Clinic, Jr. NBA Skills Challenge, Jr. NBA STEM Clinic, Adapted Sports Clinic, and a Business of Basketball event.
