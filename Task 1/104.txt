San Antonio visits Golden State, aims to stop road slide.
San Antonio Spurs (13-50, 15th in the Western Conference) vs. Golden State Warriors (33-29, ninth in the Western Conference). San Francisco; Saturday, 8:30 p.m. EST. BOTTOM LINE: San Antonio will aim to stop its seven-game road losing streak when the Spurs take on Golden State. The Warriors have gone 16-21 against Western Conference opponents. Golden State has a 4-10 record in games decided by less than 4 points. The Spurs are 8-28 against Western Conference opponents. San Antonio is 2-5 in games decided by 3 points or fewer.
