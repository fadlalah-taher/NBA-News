The 2023-24 National Basketball Association regular season might be winding down, but teams are just starting to ramp up for the home stretch of the playoff race. The only problem? A handful of contenders are dealing with long term injuries to star players. In this week's roundtable, we asked FOX Sports' NBA reporters Ric Bucher and Yaron Weitzman to predict how these injuries might affect the playoff picture, and whether there will be any lingering effects going into the 2024 offseason. The Athletic reported Thursday that Karl-Anthony Towns has a torn left meniscus and is out indefinitely.
