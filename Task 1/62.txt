Bennedict Richard Felder Mathurin is a Canadian professional basketball player for the Indiana Pacers of the National Basketball Association. He played college basketball for the Arizona Wildcats, where he was named a consensus second-team All-American and Pac-12 Player of the Year after his sophomore season. On January 2, 2021, Mathurin posted 24 points and 11 rebounds in an 86–82 win over Washington State. On January 14, he recorded 31 points and eight rebounds in a 98–64 win over Oregon State. In this season 2023-24 in NBA statistics are 14.5 points, 4.0 rebounds, 2.0 assists and 44.6 FG % per game.
