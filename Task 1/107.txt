Cedi Osman; born (8 April 1995) is a Turkish professional basketball player for the San Antonio Spurs of the National Basketball Association (NBA). He plays the small forward position. Osman was born in Ohrid, Macedonia, modern-day North Macedonia to a Turkish father and a Bosniak mother. On 25 June 2015, Osman was selected with the 31st overall pick in the 2015 NBA draft, by the Minnesota Timberwolves. His draft rights, along with those of Rakeem Christmas and a future draft pick, were then traded to the Cleveland Cavaliers, in exchange for the draft rights to Tyus Jones that same night.
