Anthony Marshon Davis Jr., is an American professional basketball player for the Los Angeles Lakers of the NBA. He previously played for the New Orleans Pelicans. He plays the power forward and center positions. Davis has appeared in the Lakers' injury report almost on a regular basis, but as has been the trend, he should be available in a tough matchup against the Bucks. Davis is averaging 24.9 points and 13.2 rebounds per game over his last 10 appearances, posting a double-double in each of those contests. Season 2023-24 statistics are 24.8 points, 12.3 rebounds and 3.6 assists per game.
