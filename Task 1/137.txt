The National Basketball Association will also host several events for fans of all ages at venues throughout Phoenix, including National Basketball Association All Star Practice presented by AT&T, the Ruffles National Basketball Association All Star Celebrity Game and an National Basketball Association All-Star fan fest, featuring basketball programming and entertainment as well as up close access to NBA players, legends and celebrities.
This will mark the fourth time Phoenix has hosted All-Star weekend, with the most recent event occurring in 2009.
