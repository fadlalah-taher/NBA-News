The National Basketball Association will host several events for fans of all ages at venues throughout Los Angeles and Inglewood, including at Kia Forum and the Los Angeles Convention Center. These events, including National Basketball Association All-Star Practice presented by AT&T, the Ruffles NBA All Star Celebrity Game and an NBA All-Star fan fest, will feature basketball programming and entertainment for fans as well as up-close access to NBA players, legends and celebrities. This will mark the record seventh NBA All-Star in the Los Angeles area, and the first time the midseason showcase has been held at the Intuit Dome.
